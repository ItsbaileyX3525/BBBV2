What is BBB?

Players online: 0

BBB, also known as club bear, is a game made by Squiggle on the ursina game engine, this introduction of this game, club bear, was the first time networking was implemented into ursina and the second I saw it I fell in love and asked Squiggle if I could modify club bear and add improvements to it like emojis, better play area and optional swear word filter

Ever since then whenever I try out a new thing that has networking built into it I try my best to recreate club_bear (or BBB) it's a very simple concept as you just handle multple people joining and update the positions and have a basic in-game chat
